Arnesh Banerjee

B.Tech in CSE (Data Science), Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata

I am an undergraduate researcher working across AI safety, NLP, and medical imaging. My research interests lie in understanding the limitations and alignment of large language models, reinforcement learning from human feedback, and developing robust methods for safety-critical AI systems. I have worked with Prof. Debotosh Bhattacharjee (Jadavpur University) and Dr. Arnob Ghosh (NJIT) on problems spanning breast thermography segmentation and safe RLHF. My work has been published at AGC 2026 and is available as pre-prints on arXiv.

Research Experience

November 2025 – Present · Remote

  • Conducted a rigorous ablation study (20 training runs) across U-Net, ResNet-only, and hybrid architectures, demonstrating generalization trade-offs in medical imaging.
  • Built a fully automated cross-validation pipeline with checkpointing, experiment logging, and reproducible training.
New Jersey Institute of Technology — Advisor: Dr. Arnob Ghosh
June 2025 – November 2025 · On-site / Virtual

  • Designed and tested a comparative 800-datapoint safety benchmark against state-of-the-art LLMs (GPT, DeepSeek, Gemini, Qwen, Mistral).
  • Demonstrated 50% higher safety compliance than ChatGPT 5 across benchmark scenarios.
Heritage Institute of Technology — Advisor: Ms. Arpita Talukdar
October 2024 – March 2025 · On-site
