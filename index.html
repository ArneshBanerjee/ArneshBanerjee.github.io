01 — About

Building at the edge of ML research

I'm a third-year B.Tech student in Data Science at Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata, working on Safer AI, LLM Reasoning, Applied ML.

My research spans medical image segmentation at Jadavpur University, Certifiable Safe RLHF at New Jersey Institute of Technology, and Recursive Feature Selection for cancer diagnostics — all resulting in peer-reviewed publications and arXiv preprints.

I care about building systems that are not just accurate, but interpretable, safe, and meaningful.
